Sisal Fiber Products

Africa's largest producer of high-quality, sustainable sisal fiber for global markets

Our Sisal Fiber

REA Vipingo Group owns and operates three premier sisal estates in Tanzania - the Mwera, Sakura and Kigombe estates, strategically located between 30 and 70 kilometers south of Tanga in northeastern Tanzania.

Our Tanzanian operations produce approximately 7,000 tonnes of premium sisal fiber annually. While a portion is used in our own Amboni sisal spinning mill, the majority is exported globally through the port of Tanga.
