Our History

REA Vipingo Plantations Limited was incorporated in Kenya in 1995 and comprises the company, which has four wholly owned subsidiaries:

  • Dwa Estate Limited – owns the Dwa estate
  • Amboni Plantations Limited – owns the three Tanzanian estates
  • Amboni Spinning Mill Limited – owns the Spinning Mill in Tanzania
  • Wigglesworth Exporters Limited – a warehousing and shipping operation based in Mombasa, Kenya

The company’s origins date back to the early 20th century, with a rich heritage in sisal cultivation and processing. Over the decades, REA Vipingo has expanded its operations across Kenya and Tanzania, becoming a key player in the global sisal industry.

Our commitment to quality, sustainability, and innovation has enabled us to maintain leadership in the market while supporting local communities and promoting environmental stewardship.

With an annual sisal fibre production of over 17,000 tonnes, the company is by far the largest sisal producer in Africa. Other than some fibre that is used in the group’s sisal spinning mill in Tanzania, all fibre produced is exported.
